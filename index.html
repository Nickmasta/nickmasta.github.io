Nicholas Chan's Portfolio

Summary

Nicholas recently graduated from the University of British Columbia in the Electrical Engineering program. Having spent most of his life in Vancouver, he is familiar with the city and the abundance of design innovation. With his experience as an electrical designer, he is familiar with the construction design process and drafting electrical drawings. Nicholas is very passionate about sustainability and renewable energy. In his studies, he has honed his knowledge of power systems and renewable technologies while also challenging himself with courses in deep learning, semiconductors, and power electronics to understand the technology which powers our world.

Nicholas hopes to bring his collaborative and hard-working attitude to the workplace with like-minded and forward-thinking peers as the world continues to electrify.
